Get to know about our company

Bright Africa Trading PLC: Was established as a commission firm to deliver technical and advisory services for international and local companies in Ethiopia.

BAT covers every area in preparation of supporting request document for clients, due diligence for financers, establishing strong linkage, organize and facilitation meetings, advising the entities to appropriate managing the finance & improving enterprises mechanism of repatriation for foreigner companies through developed international market linkages with the real buyers on the globe.

The Firm provides high-quality due diligence for international financers and investors in multiple of areas which among others include investment loan, finance, fund raise, linkage building with international market for both import and export and operational facilitation.

Moreover, it also provides short-term trainings how to manage international financing companies together the concerned government bodies; project performance evaluation and socio-economic studies to public institutions & private enterprises and Governmental Organizations by using up-to-date knowledge and state-of-art technologies.

BAT PLC manages to develop a highly dynamic, vibrant, and organization which envisions becoming one of the leading Advisory firms in Africa.
